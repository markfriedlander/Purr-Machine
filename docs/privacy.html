Privacy

Purr Machine does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

All audio and haptic playback happens entirely on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking of any kind.

The app does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or health data.

Audio plays through your device's speakers; haptics use the iPhone's Taptic engine. Nothing is transmitted anywhere.
