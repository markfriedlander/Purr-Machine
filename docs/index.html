Three real cats. Their actual recordings, looped seamlessly and layered with haptic patterns drawn from each cat's own measured rhythm. Lie down. Put the phone on your chest. Close your eyes.

Three real cats

Floozy, Nacho, and No-No!. The recordings are the actual cats — every breath, every bell jingle.

Their own rhythms

Each cat's haptic pulses at the breath cycle measured from their own recording. Nacho's is Nacho's.

Layered haptics

A continuous purr modulated by breath, with a subtle heartbeat underneath. Two patterns on the Taptic engine, at once.

Sleep timer

Ten, twenty, thirty minutes — or ∞. Fall asleep to a purring cat and the app turns itself off.

Plays under lock

Audio continues when the screen sleeps. Haptics resume the moment you wake the phone.
